2023 Ford Puma ST-Line Experience the perfect blend of style, practicality, and performance with the 2023 Ford Puma ST-Line. With its elevated ride height, the Puma offers easy entry and exit, plus a hands-free tailgate and surprisingly spacious boot-perfect for everyday convenience. Backed by the balance of Ford's 5-year unlimited kilometre new vehicle warranty, this compact SUV is a standout choice for Kiwi drivers.

Powered by Ford's EcoBoost Hybrid 48-volt technology, the Puma delivers a fun and responsive drive both in the city and on the open road. Its 1.0L three-cylinder turbocharged petrol engine produces 92 kW of power and 210 Nm of torque, while maintaining impressive fuel efficiency.

Key Features: Wireless phone charging Hands-free tailgate Keyless start Self-parking assist Front and rear parking sensors + reversing camera ISOFIX child seat anchor points Tyre pressure monitoring system Comprehensive airbag coverage (driver, passenger, side, curtain) 8-inch full-colour touchscreen with Apple CarPlay, Android Auto & FordPass app integration

ST-Line Enhancements: The ST-Line trim adds a sporty edge with: 12.3-inch digital instrument cluster Flat-bottom leather steering wheel with Race Red stitching 17-inch five-spoke alloy wheels Matte black grille, side skirts, and rear spoiler Race Red interior stitching and ST-Line sports seats

Whether you're after a stylish city cruiser or a capable weekend adventurer, the Puma ST-Line ticks all the boxes.
